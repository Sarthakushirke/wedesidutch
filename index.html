We've been Making The Delicious Foods Since 2023

From Spices to Success

Gaurri moved from India to the Netherlands with dreams in her heart and recipes in her hands. Missing the warmth of home-cooked food, she realized others felt the same. What began as a few tiffins for students soon turned into a beloved kitchen of authentic Indian flavors. With her husband by her side — her biggest cheerleader and silent strength — she turned her passion into a restaurant that now feels like home for many.
